Tuesday's recipe

Nature's Cereal

Ingredients

1 cup blueberries
1 cup raspberries
1 cup blackberries
1/4 cup pomegranate seeds
1 cup coconut water
1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint


Instructions

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and stir to combine.
Best to eat within 3 hours. Rise, shine and enjoy!
